reference: Central pattern generator inspired control for adaptive walking of biped robots

Abstract

This paper deals with adaptive walking control of biped robots. Use CPGs to generate CoG trajectory and a workspace trajectory modulation process. Also, the generator can use feedback to modulate CoG trajectories and workspace trajectories. A motion engine maps trajectories from workspace to joint space. We use NAO platform to confirm effctiveness of the proposed control strategy.

Introduction

The main contributions of this paper:

  1. A CoG trajectory generation method is proposed based on CPGs, A mapping function is designed to map the output signals of CPGs to 3-D CoG trajectories online for a biped robot.
  2. A workspace trajectory modulator is designed based on CoG trajectories. The generated CoG trajectories are used to modulate the workspace trajectories to realize adaptive walking.
  3. Through the mutual entrainment of CPGs with the feedback signals of the robot, the adaptive CoG trajectories and workspace trajectories can be generated online.
  4. The proposed control strategy is validated using the biped robot NAO.

Control Architecture


The control system architecture consists of a trajectory generator and a motion engine. The paper uses feedback information along with function mapping to generate CoG trajectory. Then the CoG output can modulate workspace generator. And finally the workspace trajectory can generate the joint signals.

CPG-Inspired Trajectory Generator

The mapping function is designed to transform the output of the CPGs to CoG trajectories. The amplitude and period of CoG trajectories can modulate the workspace trajectories. The objectives of the trajecories are as followed:

  1. Generation of CoG
  2. Generation of workspace trajectories
  3. Entraining with feedback information
